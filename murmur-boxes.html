How To Listen

  1. Anchor S1 and S2 first, then identify whether turbulence is systolic or diastolic.
  2. Classify the contour: crescendo-decrescendo, holosystolic, early decrescendo, rumble, or late systolic.
  3. Use best auscultation area and expected radiation to narrow the differential.
  4. Apply maneuvers and track which lesions increase or decrease.
  5. Confirm mechanism with the flow diagram and valve behavior.
